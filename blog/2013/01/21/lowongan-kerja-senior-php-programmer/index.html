Lowongan Kerja - Senior PHP Programmer

Jan 21st, 2013

PT. Spavista, an online company which aim to be the global spa booking, is looking for a senior php programmer to join us.
We are at the moment a team of two passionate young guys and we are currently based in Bali Island and we love surfing.

Requirements:

  • Candidate must possess at least a Bachelor’s Degree, Computer Science/Information Technology or equivalent
  • 5+ years of web development experience and passion for quality code
  • Strong OO PHP MVC development
  • Experienced in highly use of PHP DateTime
  • Experienced in how to scale PHP
  • Experience with Codeigniter Framework is a must, experience with Laravel or Symphony 2 Framework is an advantage
  • Experience with JSON format API design
  • Experience with Composer Package Management
  • Experience with Amazon Web Services is a must, because our Architecture relied heavily on Amazon
  • Experience with Github
  • Solid work ethic and willingness to dive in and get things done wherever needed
  • Full-Time position(s) available

You don’t have to be in Bali to apply for this job. Working Remote is possible. You can also move to Bali and we will help you with your accommodation.

Advantages joining Spavista :

  • Bonus
  • Equity
  • Yoga Class
  • If available, please also include your Twitter, Linked & Github.

Please send your resume to jobs@spavista.com with the subject ‘Senior Programmer’. Good luck guys
